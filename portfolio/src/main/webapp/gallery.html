Photography

During my freshamn year in college, I took a photography class. I found this class quite interesting because it was a black-and -white photography class. This meant that we had to go outdoors and take a couple shots on film then spend about four hours in the dark room developing the films. We took a variety of pictures depending on the theme of the week, portraiture, available light, cityscapes, etc. I kept an album of some of my favorite shots from the class, four of which I've shared below. On a random day, I enjoy photographing strutures with unique designs, beatiful sceneries, and random pictures of my friends.
